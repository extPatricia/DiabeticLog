## DiabeticLog

This mobile application is designed to improve the quality of life of people living with diabetes, especially those with Type 1 Diabetes Mellitus.
Its main goal is to simplify the daily tracking and management of essential health data, helping users maintain better control over their condition.

The app centralizes all relevant information in one place, reducing mistakes, forgotten entries, and improving treatment adherence.
